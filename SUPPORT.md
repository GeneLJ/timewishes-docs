# Support

## About

timeWishes is a fully offline application. All data is stored locally on your device. There is no cloud sync, no account system, and no backend.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored in a local SwiftData database on your device. Uninstalling the app, resetting your device, or manually clearing app data will permanently erase this content. It cannot be recovered.

### How can I back up my data?
The app does not currently offer a built-in backup feature. We recommend performing regular device-wide backups through Finder (macOS) or iTunes / iCloud, which include app data.

### How do I delete all my data?
Open the app, go to Settings → Danger Zone → Clear All Data. Alternatively, uninstall the app.

### Which languages are supported?
Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Arabic, German, French, Spanish, and Portuguese — nine languages total. You can switch language in Settings.

### Why does the app compute a "life countdown"?
"Life expectancy" is a setting that lets you consciously reflect on the length of a life and use time more intentionally. **It is not a medical prediction.** The default is 80 years; you may set any value between 1 and 100.

### Can I reset or delete a person or wish?
Yes. Swipe left on a list row to delete it, or open the detail view and use the Delete button. Deletion cannot be undone.

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?
Settings → Appearance → Light / Dark. The default is Dark.

## Known Limitations

- The app is built with the iOS 26.1 SDK and requires iOS 26.1 or later.
- The app runs only on iPhone (iPad is not supported).
- The app supports portrait orientation only.
- The app does not request network access and does not provide sync or cloud backup.
