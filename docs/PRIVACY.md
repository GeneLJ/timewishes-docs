# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

## Overview

timeWishes is a personal countdown and wish-tracking application that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Storage

All data you enter in the app — including your nickname, birthday, life expectancy, important people, wishes, and achievements — is stored **locally on your device** using SwiftData. The app does not upload any of this data to a server. The developer has no access to your data.

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

## Data Collection

The app **does not collect any data**. It does not track users, and it does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or social SDKs.

## System API Usage

The app uses the system `UserDefaults` framework solely to store your local preferences (dark mode setting, selected language, first-launch state). This usage is disclosed in `Resources/PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy` under reason `CA92.1`.

## Encryption

- The app does not use any custom encryption library.
- It does not use any non-exempt encryption. The `ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption` flag is set to `NO` in the app's `Info.plist`.
- iOS-standard Data Protection applies automatically to local storage.

## Third-Party Services

None. The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, push notifications, advertising, or social sharing.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is materially updated, the change will be reflected on this page and shipped with a new app version.
